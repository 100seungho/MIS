🎶 2020 Top 3 most-streamed songs

I am using YouTube Premium as a music streaming service. At the end of last year, YouTube made a playlist with the songs I listened to the most during the year. Below are the songs I listened to the most last year on YouTube.

오랜만에, 죠지 

As the city pop boom has been made in Korea since some time ago, I have heard a lot of city pop last year. With the city pop boom, Kim Hyun-chul, who is evaluated for bringing city pop into Korea, released "오랜만에" in 1989, was also remade after being re-examined by a singer named George. The original song is good, but I listened to it a lot last year because of the sophisticated arrangement.

 

Watermelon Sugar, Harry Styles

It is a song that became famous due to twitch streamer Judasa. I listened to it once because I was attracted by YouTube algorithms, and I have listened to it a lot since then. I don't watch the streamer's broadcasts, but I like the playlist, so I often search for "Judasa Playlist" and listen to music.

Minsu is confuesed (민수는 혼란스럽다, 민수)

I don't know how I got to know this song, but at some point, it was coming out of my ears and laughed loudly at the title. Maybe I found out while listening to other songs on the "On Stage" YouTube channel. Regardless of the original title, I listened to the song a lot because it was so good. Actually, I didn't know that I heard this much.
